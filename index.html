The NIAMT Racing Club is a student-driven initiative at the National Institute of Advanced Manufacturing Technology (NIAMT), Ranchi. We focus on designing and developing cutting-edge racing vehicles. Founded by passionate engineering students, our club embodies the values of innovation, teamwork, and sustainability. Founded in 2011, the club runs the official college chapter of the SAE Collegiate Club at NIAMT.

The 'Team THRUSTERS' have brought laurels to the institute by winning and showing exemplary performance in many Asian, European, and Indian competitions. Over the years, the team has grown into a tightly-knit unit of engineers who turn ideas into high-performance machines. With each season, we strive to push the boundaries of student motorsport and represent India on global platforms.

Our Engineering Domains

Designed to Win

Our design team uses advanced CAD tools to create aerodynamic, balanced, and structurally sound vehicles built for performance.

Mastering the Machine

We specialize in designing robust and lightweight chassis structures that ensure safety, stiffness, and high-speed stability on the track.

Powering Performance

From engine tuning to power delivery, our powertrain systems are designed for maximum output and efficiency under race conditions.

Braking with Precision

Our braking system ensures quick response and heat resistance, enabling sharp cornering and confident control in every lap.

Our Legacy

NIAMT Racing Club has always been more than just a team — it’s a legacy built on passion, resilience, and innovation. Over the years, we’ve grown from a small group of enthusiasts into a competitive force representing our institute on national and international platforms. Every build, every race, and every result is a chapter in our legacy — where we not only chase speed but also perfection in design, engineering, and teamwork. We don't just build cars. We build the future, one race at a time.
