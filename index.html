NIAMT STUDENT RACING CLUB

National Institute of Advanced Manufacturing Technology, Ranchi

The NIAMT Racing Club is a student-driven initiative at the National Institute of Advanced Manufacturing Technology (NIAMT), Ranchi. We focus on designing and developing of racing cutting-edge vehicles. Founded by passionate engineering students, our club embodies the values of innovation, teamwork, and sustainability.
Founded in 2011, the club runs the college chapter of SAE collegiate club at NIAMT. The team 'THRUSTERS' also participated in the Formula SAE (FSAE Italy) 2013 events and competed with teams from all over the world. The teams have brought laurels to the institute by winning and showing exemplary performance in many Asian, European and Indian competitions More than just a team, the NIAMT Racing Club is a community of like-minded individuals driven by a passion for engineering and innovation. We are always looking for enthusiastic students who share our vision and are eager to contribute to the future of sustainable transportation.

Our Engineering Domains

Designed to Win

Our design team uses advanced CAD tools to create aerodynamic, balanced, and structurally sound vehicles built for performance.

Mastering the Machine

We specialize in designing robust and lightweight chassis structures that ensure safety, stiffness, and high-speed stability on the track.

Powering Performance

From engine tuning to power delivery, our powertrain systems are designed for maximum output and efficiency under race conditions.

Braking with Precision

Our braking system ensures quick response and heat resistance, enabling sharp cornering and confident control in every lap.
